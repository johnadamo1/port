Jordan 1 "Chicago"

The shoe that started it all

Pictured above, the Air Jordan 1 "Chicago" orginaly worn by Micheal Jordan as he played for the Bulls.

A list of the times this shoe has been released:

  • 1985 - orignal release date for the Jordan 1 Chicago.
  • 1994 - first retro of the shoe. Today these are the oldest wearable pairs.
  • 2013 - 19 years following the previous retro, this was the most pairs ever produced.
  • 2015 - a realease with less volume than the 2013, but the most recent re-release of the shoe.
